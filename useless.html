It Was Useless

I once visited a cosmetics store with a buddy to get hair accessories. At the time, I was actually getting ready for my birthday and intended to visit the studio that day. So, as soon as I walked in, I asked one of the staff members whether they had the specific one I needed. Yes, she replied. I quickly took it and paid for it once she showed me where it was kept because that was just what I needed. I just took it and walked out of the store without trying it on. However, guess what? I had just finished getting ready when I arrived to the studio. I took it and intended to use it, but lo and behold, it was broader than my head and didn't fit my hair. I was astonished because I had no idea that it came in different sizes, but I had to use it that way. After my birthday, I gave it to someone; however, when I saw it on her head, I discovered it was large and that she had only used it once before not using it again. When I realized what I had purchased with my own money, it was useless. Have you ever purchased something that turned out to be useless for you?
